Precision

Nearly four decades
of disciplined craft.

Greg has led the construction of luxury custom homes, destination resorts, hotels, medical facilities, retail developments, and large-scale commercial projects. Those experiences shaped the disciplined approach, technical expertise, and unwavering standards that define every Vantage home today.

Today, Greg brings that experience to Overland Park and the greater Kansas City region through Vantage Homes — a company founded on the belief that extraordinary homes are the result of exceptional craftsmanship, timeless architecture, and an uncompromising commitment to quality.
